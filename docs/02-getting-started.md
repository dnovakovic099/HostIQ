# Getting Started with HostIQ

This guide will walk you through downloading, installing, and setting up your HostIQ account.

## Download & Installation

### iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1. Open the **App Store** on your device
2. Search for **"HostIQ"**
3. Tap **"Get"** or **"Install"**
4. Wait for the installation to complete
5. Tap the HostIQ icon to open the app

**Requirements:**
- iOS 14.0 or later
- iPhone or iPad

### Android

1. Open **Google Play Store** on your device
2. Search for **"HostIQ"**
3. Tap **"Install"**
4. Wait for the installation to complete
5. Tap the HostIQ icon to open the app

**Requirements:**
- Android 5.0 or later
- Google Play Services

## Creating Your Account

### Option 1: Email Sign-Up

1. Open the HostIQ app
2. Tap **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Select your role:
   - **Property Owner** - If you own/manage properties
   - **Cleaner** - If you perform cleaning services
5. Tap **"Create Account"**
6. Check your email for verification (if required)
7. Enter the verification code when prompted

### Option 2: Google Sign-In

1. Open the HostIQ app
2. Tap **"Sign in with Google"**
3. Select your Google account
4. Grant permissions when prompted
5. Your account will be created automatically

## First-Time Setup

### For Property Owners

1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your name and contact information
   - Upload a profile photo (optional)

2. **Add Your First Property**
   - Tap the **"Properties"** tab
   - Tap the **"+"** button
   - Enter property details:
     - Property name
     - Address
     - Number of units
   - Tap **"Save"**

3. **Set Up Rooms**
   - Open your property
   - Add units if needed
   - Create room templates for each unit
   - Define inspection requirements

4. **Invite Cleaners**
   - Go to **"Team"** or **"Manage Cleaners"**
   - Tap **"Invite Cleaner"**
   - Enter cleaner's email address
   - They'll receive an invitation email

### For Cleaners

1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your name and contact information
   - Upload a profile photo (optional)

2. **Wait for Assignments**
   - Property owners will assign inspections to you
   - You'll see assignments on your home screen

3. **Or Create Manual Inspections**
   - Tap the **"+"** button on your home screen
   - Select a property and unit
   - Start capturing photos

## Permissions Setup

HostIQ needs certain permissions to function properly:

### Camera Permission
- **Why**: To capture inspection photos and videos
- **How**: Tap **"Allow"** when prompted
- **Can change later**: Settings → HostIQ → Camera

### Photo Library Permission
- **Why**: To select existing photos for inspections
- **How**: Tap **"Allow"** when prompted
- **Can change later**: Settings → HostIQ → Photos

### Notifications (Optional)
- **Why**: To receive assignment updates and inspection results
- **How**: Tap **"Allow"** when prompted
- **Can change later**: Settings → HostIQ → Notifications

## Biometric Authentication (Optional)

Enable Face ID (iOS) or Fingerprint (Android) for quick login:

1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Tap **"Security"** or **"Biometric Login"**
3. Toggle **"Enable Biometric Login"**
4. Follow the on-screen instructions

## Understanding Your Dashboard

### Property Owner Dashboard
- **Welcome Section**: Quick stats and overview
- **Recent Inspections**: Latest inspection results
- **Quick Actions**: Add property, manage team, view insights
- **Usage Indicator**: Track your subscription usage

### Cleaner Dashboard
- **Today's Jobs**: Assigned inspections
- **In Progress**: Inspections you've started
- **History**: Past completed inspections
- **Create Inspection**: Manual inspection button

## Troubleshooting Installation

### App Won't Install

**iOS:**
- Check you have iOS 14.0 or later
- Ensure you have enough storage space
- Try restarting your device

**Android:**
- Check you have Android 5.0 or later
- Ensure Google Play Services is updated
- Clear Play Store cache: Settings → Apps → Play Store → Clear Cache

### Can't Sign In

- Verify your email address is correct
- Check your internet connection
- Try resetting your password
- Contact support if issues persist

### Permissions Not Working

- Go to device Settings
- Find HostIQ in the app list
- Enable Camera and Photos permissions
- Restart the app
