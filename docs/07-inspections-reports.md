# Inspections & Reports Guide

Learn how to view, understand, and use inspection results and reports in HostIQ.

## Overview

After cleaners submit inspections, AI analyzes the photos and generates detailed reports. This guide explains how to access, understand, and use these reports.

## Viewing Inspections

### From Owner Dashboard

1. **Recent Inspections Section**
   - See latest inspections
   - Tap any inspection card
   - View full details

2. **Inspection Reports Tab**
   - Go to Inspections tab
   - See all inspections
   - Filter and search

### From Properties

1. Open a property
2. Go to **"Inspections"** section
3. See property-specific inspections
4. Tap to view details

## Inspection Details

### Overview Section

**Basic Information:**
- Property and unit name
- Cleaner who performed inspection
- Date and time
- Status (Complete, Processing, Failed)

**Scores:**
- Cleanliness Score (0-10)
- Letter Grade (A+ to F)
- Guest-Ready Status
- Overall Assessment

### Status Indicators

**Complete:**
- ✅ Green indicator
- Analysis finished
- Full report available

**Processing:**
- ⏳ Orange indicator
- AI analysis in progress
- Wait for completion

**Failed:**
- ❌ Red indicator
- Didn't meet standards
- Review issues

## Understanding Scores

### Cleanliness Score (0-10)

**Scale:**
- **9.0-10.0**: Excellent (rare)
- **8.0-8.9**: Very Good
- **7.0-7.9**: Good
- **6.0-6.9**: Acceptable
- **5.0-5.9**: Mediocre
- **4.0-4.9**: Below Average
- **3.0-3.9**: Poor
- **Below 3.0**: Unacceptable

**What It Means:**
- Overall cleanliness assessment
- Based on AI analysis
- 5-star hotel standards
- Objective measurement

### Letter Grades

- **A+** (9.5-10.0): Theoretical perfection
- **A** (9.0-9.5): Virtually flawless
- **A-** (8.5-9.0): Extremely rare
- **B+** (8.0-8.5): Very good
- **B** (7.0-8.0): Good
- **B-** (6.0-7.0): Decent
- **C+** (5.0-6.0): Mediocre
- **C** (4.0-5.0): Poor
- **C-** (3.0-4.0): Bad
- **D** (2.0-3.0): Terrible
- **F** (0.0-2.0): Fail

### Guest-Ready Status

**Ready (✅):**
- Meets 5-star standards
- Suitable for guests
- No major issues

**Not Ready (❌):**
- Needs attention
- Issues found
- Not guest-ready
