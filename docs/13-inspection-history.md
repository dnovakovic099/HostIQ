# Inspection History - Cleaner Guide

View and learn from your past inspections to improve your performance.

## Overview

The Inspection History screen shows all your completed inspections, allowing you to review your work, check scores, and learn from feedback.

## Accessing History

### From Dashboard

1. Open HostIQ app
2. Tap **"History"** tab (bottom navigation)
3. See all past inspections

### From Home Screen

1. Go to home screen
2. Tap **"History"** or **"Reports"** tab
3. View inspection list
