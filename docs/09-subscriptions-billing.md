# Subscriptions & Billing Guide

Learn about HostIQ subscription plans, billing, and usage management.

## Overview

HostIQ offers flexible subscription plans to meet different needs. This guide explains how subscriptions work and how to manage your billing.

## Understanding Subscriptions

### What's Included

Subscriptions typically include:
- **Inspections**: Number of AI-analyzed inspections per month
- **Features**: Access to premium features
- **Support**: Priority customer support
- **Storage**: Photo and media storage

### Usage Limits

- **Free Tier**: Limited inspections (typically 10 images)
- **Paid Plans**: More inspections included
- **Overage**: Pay per additional inspection
- **Reset**: Limits reset monthly

## Viewing Your Subscription

### From Dashboard

1. Open HostIQ app
2. Look at **Usage Indicator** (top of dashboard)
3. See current usage and limits

### From Settings

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **"Billing"** or **"Subscription"**
3. View:
   - Current plan
   - Usage statistics
   - Billing history
   - Available plans

## Subscription Plans

### Plan Features

Each plan includes:
- **Monthly Inspections**: Number included
- **Overage Price**: Cost per additional inspection
- **Features**: Available functionality
- **Support**: Support level

### Comparing Plans

**Basic Plan:**
- Lower monthly cost
- Fewer inspections
- Essential features

**Premium Plan:**
- Higher monthly cost
- More inspections
- Advanced features

**Enterprise Plan:**
- Highest tier
- Unlimited or high limits
- All features included

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View available plans
3. Select higher plan
4. Complete payment
5. Upgrade takes effect immediately

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View current plan
3. Select lower plan
4. Changes take effect at next billing cycle

### Switching Plans

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View all plans
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm switch
5. Prorated billing applies

## Payment Methods

### iOS (App Store)

- **Payment**: Through Apple App Store
- **Methods**: Credit card, Apple Pay, iTunes balance
- **Management**: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions

### Android (Google Play)

- **Payment**: Through Google Play Store
- **Methods**: Credit card, Google Pay, Play balance
- **Management**: Play Store → Subscriptions

3. See past invoices
4. Download receipts

**Overage Charges:**

**One-Time Purchases:**
- Additional inspections

## Free Tier

### What's Included

- **Limited Inspections**: Typically 10 images
- **Basic Features**: Core functionality
- **Standard Support**: Email support

### Limitations

- **Usage Limits**: Restricted inspections
- **Features**: Some features unavailable
- **Storage**: Limited media storage

### Upgrading from Free

1. Reach usage limit
2. See upgrade prompt
3. Select plan
4. Complete payment
5. Immediate access

## Overage & Additional Usage

### What is Overage?

Overage = Inspections beyond your plan limit

**Example:**
- Plan includes 50 inspections/month
- You use 60 inspections
- 10 inspections = overage
- Charged per overage inspection

### Overage Pricing

- **Per Inspection**: Fixed price per additional
- **Billing**: End of month
- **No Cap**: Can exceed significantly

### Managing Overage

**Options:**
1. **Monitor Usage**: Track throughout month
2. **Upgrade Plan**: Get more included
3. **Reduce Usage**: Use fewer inspections
4. **Accept Overage**: Pay for additional

## Canceling Subscription

### How to Cancel

**iOS:**
1. Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
2. Find HostIQ
3. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

**Android:**
1. Play Store → Subscriptions
2. Find HostIQ
3. Tap "Cancel"
4. Confirm cancellation

### After Cancellation

- **Access**: Continues until period ends
- **Features**: Limited after period
- **Data**: Preserved for 30 days
- **Reactivation**: Can resubscribe anytime

## Refunds

### Refund Policy

- **App Store/Play Store**: Follows platform policy
- **Request**: Through platform
- **Processing**: 5-10 business days
- **Eligibility**: Varies by situation

### Requesting Refund

**iOS:**
- reportaproblem.apple.com
- Select HostIQ
- Choose reason
- Submit request

**Android:**
- play.google.com/store/account
- Order history
- Request refund
- Select reason

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed

**Check:**
- Payment method valid
- Sufficient funds
- Card not expired
- Billing address correct

**Solutions:**
- Update payment method
- Contact bank/card issuer
- Try different payment method
- Contact support

### Can't Access Features

**Check:**
- Subscription is active
- Plan includes feature
- Usage limit not exceeded
- App is updated

**Solutions:**
- Verify subscription status
- Check plan details
- Upgrade if needed
- Contact support

### Wrong Charges

**Check:**
- Billing history
- Plan details
- Overage usage
- Promotional pricing

**Solutions:**
- Review charges
- Contact billing support
- Request clarification
- Dispute if incorrect
