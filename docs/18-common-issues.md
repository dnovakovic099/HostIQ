# Common Issues & Solutions

This guide helps you troubleshoot common problems in HostIQ.

## Installation & Setup

### App Won't Install

**iOS:**
- ✅ Check iOS version (need 14.0+)
- ✅ Ensure enough storage space
- ✅ Restart device and try again
- ✅ Update iOS if outdated

**Android:**
- ✅ Check Android version (need 5.0+)
- ✅ Update Google Play Services
- ✅ Clear Play Store cache
- ✅ Restart device

### Can't Create Account

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Verify email format is correct
- ✅ Try different email address
- ✅ Use Google Sign-In as alternative
- ✅ Check if account already exists

### Email Verification Not Received

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Check spam/junk folder
- ✅ Wait a few minutes
- ✅ Request new verification code
- ✅ Verify email address is correct
- ✅ Check email server settings

## Camera & Photos

### Camera Permission Denied

**Fix:**
1. Go to device **Settings**
2. Find **HostIQ** in app list
3. Enable **Camera** permission
4. Restart the app

**iOS:**
- Settings → Privacy → Camera → HostIQ

**Android:**
- Settings → Apps → HostIQ → Permissions → Camera

### Photos Won't Capture

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Grant camera permission
- ✅ Restart the app
- ✅ Restart your device
- ✅ Check if camera works in other apps
- ✅ Update the app

### Photos Are Blurry

**Tips:**
- ✅ Clean camera lens
- ✅ Ensure good lighting
- ✅ Hold device steady
- ✅ Wait for focus before capturing
- ✅ Use flash if needed

### Can't Select Photos from Library

**Fix:**
1. Go to device **Settings**
2. Find **HostIQ**
3. Enable **Photos** permission
4. Restart the app

## Upload & Submission

### Photos Won't Upload

**Check:**
- ✅ Internet connection (Wi-Fi or data)
- ✅ Signal strength
- ✅ Try Wi-Fi instead of cellular
- ✅ Restart upload
- ✅ Check storage space

**Solutions:**
- Switch to better network
- Wait and retry
- Close other apps using internet
- Restart the app

### Upload Stuck or Slow

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Check internet speed
- ✅ Use Wi-Fi for large uploads
- ✅ Reduce number of photos
- ✅ Wait for current upload to finish
- ✅ Don't close app during upload

### Submission Failed

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Verify all photos uploaded
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Retry submission
- ✅ Ensure inspection has photos
- ✅ Contact support if persistent

## Inspection Issues

### Low Coverage Warning

**Meaning:** Photos don't show enough of the room

**Fix:**
- ✅ Take wider shots
- ✅ Show multiple walls
- ✅ Include more of the room
- ✅ Use corner angles
- ✅ Aim for 80%+ coverage

### Inspection Stuck Processing

**Normal:** Processing takes 1-3 minutes

**If longer:**
- ✅ Wait a few more minutes
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Refresh the screen
- ✅ Contact support if >10 minutes

### Can't Start Inspection

**Check:**
- ✅ Property has rooms set up
- ✅ You have assignment or permission
- ✅ App is updated
- ✅ Internet connection

**Solutions:**
- Wait for owner to add rooms
- Request assignment from owner
- Update the app
- Restart the app

### Inspection Score Seems Wrong

**Remember:**
- AI uses strict 5-star hotel standards
- Scores are objective and consistent
- Review detailed feedback
- Improve photos and cleanliness

**If truly incorrect:**
- Contact support with details
- Include inspection ID
- Provide specific concerns
