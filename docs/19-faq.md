# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is HostIQ?

HostIQ is a mobile application for property inspection and cleanliness verification. It uses AI to analyze inspection photos and provide objective cleanliness scores and detailed reports.

### Who can use HostIQ?

- **Property Owners**: Manage properties, assign cleaners, review inspections
- **Cleaners**: Perform inspections, capture photos, submit for analysis

### Is HostIQ free?

HostIQ offers subscription plans with different features and usage limits. Check the [Subscriptions & Billing](./09-subscriptions-billing.md) guide for details.

### What devices are supported?

- **iOS**: iPhone and iPad (iOS 14.0+)
- **Android**: Android phones and tablets (Android 5.0+)

## Account & Setup

### How do I create an account?

You can sign up with:
- Email and password
- Google Sign-In

See [Getting Started Guide](./02-getting-started.md) for details.

### Can I have both Owner and Cleaner access?

Currently, each account has one role. You can:
- Create separate accounts for each role
- Contact support for dual-role access

### How do I change my password?

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **"Change Password"**
3. Enter current and new password
4. Save changes

### Can I use biometric login?

Yes! Enable Face ID (iOS) or Fingerprint (Android) in Settings → Security.

## Inspections

### How long does AI analysis take?

Typically 1-3 minutes, depending on:
- Number of photos
- Photo quality
- Server load

### What if my inspection fails?

- Review the detailed feedback
- Address identified issues
- Retake photos if needed
- Resubmit for analysis

### How many photos should I take?

- **Minimum**: 1 per room
- **Recommended**: 3-5 per room
- **Focus**: Good coverage (80%+ of room)

### What photo quality is required?

- Clear and in focus
- Good lighting
- Wide coverage (80%+)
- Multiple angles recommended

### Can I add photos after submission?

No, but you can:
- Create a new inspection
- Add photos before submitting
- Review carefully before submitting

## Scores & Ratings

### What do the scores mean?

- **0-10 Scale**: 10 is perfect
- **Letter Grades**: A+ to F
- **Guest-Ready**: Yes/No status

See [AI Analysis Guide](./14-ai-analysis.md) for details.

### Why are scores so strict?

The AI uses 5-star hotel standards to ensure consistent, high-quality results that protect your guest experience.

### How can I improve my scores?

- Take better photos (wider coverage)
- Address cleanliness issues
- Remove clutter
- Follow room checklists
- Pay attention to details

### What's a good score?

- **8.0+**: Excellent (rare)
- **7.0-7.9**: Good
- **6.0-6.9**: Acceptable
- **Below 6.0**: Needs improvement

## Properties & Management

### How do I add a property?

1. Go to **Properties** tab
2. Tap **"+"** button
3. Enter property details
4. Add units and rooms

See [Property Management Guide](./05-property-management.md).

### Can I manage multiple properties?

Yes! HostIQ supports unlimited properties (based on your subscription).

### How do I invite cleaners?

1. Go to **Team** or **Manage Cleaners**
2. Tap **"Invite Cleaner"**
3. Enter email address
4. Cleaner receives invitation

### Can cleaners see other properties?

Cleaners only see properties they're assigned to or have access to.

## Subscriptions & Billing

### What subscription plans are available?

Plans vary by:
- Number of inspections included
- Features available
- Usage limits

Check the app's Billing screen for current plans.

### How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View available plans
3. Select new plan
4. Complete payment

### What happens if I exceed my limit?

- You'll be notified
- Can purchase additional inspections
- Or upgrade your plan

### Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime:
- iOS: Through App Store subscriptions
- Android: Through Google Play subscriptions

## Technical Issues

### The app won't open

- Restart your device
- Update the app
- Reinstall if needed
- Check device compatibility

### Photos won't upload

- Check internet connection
- Try Wi-Fi instead of data
- Restart the app
- Clear app cache

### Camera not working

- Check app permissions (Settings → HostIQ → Camera)
- Restart the app
- Restart your device
- Update the app

### Can't sign in

- Verify email/password
- Check internet connection
- Try "Forgot Password"
- Contact support

### App is slow

- Check internet connection
- Close other apps
- Restart the app
- Update to latest version
