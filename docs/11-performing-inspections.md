# Performing Inspections - Cleaner Guide

This guide walks you through the complete inspection process from start to finish.

## Overview

An inspection involves:
1. Starting an inspection
2. Capturing photos/videos of each room
3. Uploading media
4. Submitting for AI analysis
5. Reviewing results

## Starting an Inspection

### From Assignment

1. **View Your Jobs**
   - Open the app
   - Check your home screen
   - See pending assignments

2. **Select an Assignment**
   - Tap on a job card
   - Review property and unit details
   - Check due date and notes

3. **Start Inspection**
   - Tap **"Start Inspection"** button
   - System creates inspection record
   - Navigate to capture screen

### Manual Inspection

1. **Create New Inspection**
   - Tap the **"+"** button (bottom right)
   - Select property and unit
   - Tap **"Create Inspection"**

2. **Select Property**
   - Choose from your assigned properties
   - Or select any available property

3. **Select Unit**
   - Choose the specific unit
   - Verify unit details

## Room-by-Room Capture

### Understanding the Flow

The app guides you through each room systematically:

1. **Room Selection**
   - See list of rooms for the unit
   - Rooms are checked off as you complete them
   - Can skip rooms if needed

2. **Capture Media**
   - Take photos of the room
   - Capture multiple angles
   - Ensure good coverage

3. **Review & Continue**
   - Review captured photos
   - Add more if needed
   - Move to next room

### Room Types

Common room types include:
- **Bedroom**: Beds, furniture, closets
- **Bathroom**: Fixtures, cleanliness, supplies
- **Kitchen**: Appliances, counters, cleanliness
- **Living Room**: Furniture, entertainment, decor
- **Dining Room**: Table, chairs, settings
- **Hallway**: Corridors, lighting, cleanliness
- **Other**: Balconies, laundry, storage

## Capturing Photos

### Best Practices

1. **Coverage**
   - Capture 80%+ of the room
   - Show multiple walls
   - Include floor and ceiling views

2. **Lighting**
   - Use natural light when possible
   - Avoid harsh shadows
   - Ensure good visibility

3. **Angles**
   - Take corner shots (shows 2-3 walls)
   - Include wide shots
   - Add detail shots for issues

4. **Quality**
   - Keep camera steady
   - Focus clearly
   - Avoid blurry photos

### Photo Requirements

- **Minimum**: 1 photo per room
- **Recommended**: 3-5 photos per room
- **Maximum**: No strict limit
- **Format**: JPG/PNG
- **Size**: Auto-compressed by app

### What to Capture

**Always Include:**
- Overall room view
- All major furniture
- Floors and surfaces
- Windows and doors
- Any issues or damage

**Focus On:**
- Cleanliness issues
- Damage or wear
- Missing items
- Out-of-place items
- Guest-readiness

## Capturing Videos

### When to Use Video

- Show room walkthrough
- Demonstrate functionality
- Document moving parts
- Provide context

### Video Guidelines

- **Length**: 10-30 seconds recommended
- **Quality**: Clear and steady
- **Content**: Show room comprehensively
- **Audio**: Optional narration

## Uploading Media

### During Capture

1. **Review Photos**
   - See thumbnails of captured media
   - Delete unwanted photos
   - Retake if needed

2. **Continue to Next Room**
   - Tap "Next Room" or "Continue"
   - Media uploads in background
   - Can continue while uploading

### After Capture

1. **Review All Media**
   - See all uploaded photos
   - Check room coverage
   - Verify quality

2. **Add More Media**
   - Can add photos to any room
   - Select from library
   - Take additional photos

## Submitting for Analysis

### Pre-Submission Checklist

Before submitting, verify:
- ✅ All rooms captured
- ✅ Photos are clear and in focus
- ✅ Good room coverage (80%+)
- ✅ Issues documented
- ✅ Media uploaded successfully

### Submission Process

1. **Review Screen**
   - See summary of inspection
   - Review all captured media
   - Check room completion

2. **Submit Button**
   - Tap **"Submit for Analysis"**
   - Confirm submission
   - Wait for processing

3. **Processing Status**
   - Shows "Processing" status
   - AI analyzes photos
   - Usually takes 1-3 minutes

## Understanding Results

### Cleanliness Score

- **Scale**: 0-10 (10 is perfect)
- **Grade**: Letter grade (A+ to F)
- **Meaning**: Overall cleanliness assessment

### Guest-Ready Status

- **Ready**: ✅ Meets standards
- **Not Ready**: ❌ Needs attention
- **Meaning**: Whether property is guest-ready

### Issues Found

- **Severity**: Low, Medium, High, Critical
- **Categories**: Cleanliness, Damage, Missing Items
- **Details**: Specific issues identified

### Room Analysis

Each room gets:
- Individual score
- Issue list
- Recommendations
- Photo references

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working

- Check app permissions
- Restart the app
- Restart your device
- Use photo library as backup

### Upload Failing

- Check internet connection
- Try Wi-Fi instead of data
- Retry upload
- Contact support if persistent

### Low Coverage Warning

- Take wider shots
- Include more of the room
- Show multiple walls
- Add corner shots

### Submission Errors

- Verify all media uploaded
- Check internet connection
- Retry submission
- Contact support if needed
