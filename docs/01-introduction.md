# Introduction to HostIQ

## What is HostIQ?

HostIQ is a powerful mobile application designed for short-term rental property management. It streamlines the inspection process, ensures consistent quality standards, and provides actionable insights through AI-powered analysis.

## Who is HostIQ For?

### Property Owners & Managers
HostIQ helps you:
- Maintain consistent quality across all your properties
- Track cleaner performance and identify issues early
- Generate professional inspection reports
- Manage multiple properties and teams efficiently
- Make data-driven decisions with analytics

### Cleaners & Cleaning Teams
HostIQ helps you:
- Complete inspections quickly and efficiently
- Document your work with photos and videos
- Get clear feedback on your performance
- Track your assignments and history
- Ensure you meet quality standards

## Core Capabilities

### 1. AI-Powered Inspection Analysis
- Automated cleanliness scoring (1-10 scale)
- Issue detection and categorization
- Guest-readiness assessment
- Damage and inventory tracking
- Professional grade analysis

### 2. Comprehensive Media Capture
- High-quality photo capture
- Video recording support
- Room-by-room documentation
- Multiple media upload per inspection
- Automatic cloud storage

### 3. Property Management
- Multi-property support
- Unit and room management
- Custom room templates
- Inventory tracking
- Valuable items management

### 4. Team Collaboration
- Cleaner assignment system
- Real-time status updates
- Performance tracking
- Team communication
- Invitation system

### 5. Analytics & Reporting
- Property performance scores
- Cleaner performance metrics
- Inspection history
- Trend analysis
- Exportable reports

## How It Works

### For Property Owners

1. **Setup**: Add your properties and define room templates
2. **Assign**: Invite cleaners and assign inspections
3. **Review**: Receive AI-analyzed inspection reports
4. **Act**: Use insights to improve quality and performance

### For Cleaners

1. **Receive**: Get assigned inspections or create new ones
2. **Capture**: Take photos and videos of each room
3. **Submit**: Upload media for AI analysis
4. **Review**: Check your scores and feedback
